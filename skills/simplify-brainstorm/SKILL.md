# simplify-brainstorm

Generate options, compare tradeoffs, and narrow the design before code.

## When To Use

- Alternatives.
- Design options.
- Architecture tradeoffs.
- Pre-implementation critique.
- Idea shaping before code.

## Core Rules

- Provide 2-3 viable approaches.
- Recommend one option and explain why.
- Keep focus on the current decision.
- When the task becomes deep, structured research and autoresearch is available, stop local brainstorming and hand it off.

## Boundaries

- Do not drift into issue orchestration.
- Do not pretend to be a research engine when autoresearch is the better fit.
